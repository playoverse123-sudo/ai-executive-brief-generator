# Design Rationale

This system is designed to ensure:

## 1. Output Consistency
Strict formatting rules eliminate variation in structure.

## 2. Insight Separation
Mutually exclusive constraints reduce redundancy and overlap.

## 3. Executive Usability
All outputs are tailored for strategic decision-making.

## 4. Deterministic Behavior
Fallback rules prevent hallucination when data is insufficient.

## 5. Real-World Application
Applicable to consulting, business intelligence, and research synthesis workflows.
